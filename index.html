About Me

I'm a second-year student at Texas State University, triple majoring in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering: Computer Engineering, and Applied Mathematics. I have experience in robotics, electronics, software development, database management, and automation. I'm passionate about designing, creating, and integrating unique software and hardware systems to solve real problems. In the past, I've worked on a variety of projects, including designing and building a omnidirectional drivetrains for large mobile robots, developing software to automate database cleanup and verification, and creating custom electrical and software systems for robotics applications.

In my free time, I enjoy designing electrical systems, developing software, baking, and playing board and video games. I love teaching others my knowledge and skills and getting new people involved in robotics. I'm currently leading a Sumo Bot team at Texas State University, teaching members that are new to robotics the basics of robot design and programming. I'm always looking for new opportunities to grow my skills while making an impact on the world around me.
